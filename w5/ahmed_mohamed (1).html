About Me

Hey, I'm **Ahmed Mohamed**, 23 years old, and originally from Sudan - Khartoum. After the war, I moved to Turkey and started my university education here. I am currently a **3rd Year MIS (Management Information System) student** at Halic University in Istanbul, Turkey.

I always wanted to study Dentistry, but due to the war in Sudan, I wasn't able to. When I started MIS, the first year was the hardest. However, starting from this third year, I've managed to cope, develop some knowledge, and better understand the department.

Nevertheless, while this may not have been the path I initially envisioned, I made a deal with myself that nothing in this life will ever be able to drag me down. I managed to succeed, and although it's hard, nothing worthwhile is easy. You either find a way out or get stuck and live in regret. For me, I always find a way out to keep pushing.

Lastly, this is my story, and I hope I keep pushing until I reach a place where I am happy with myself. My advice for anyone is to **never give up no matter what happens in life. "Be Positive, Be Strong."**
